BMW (Bayerische Motoren Werke) is a brand with over a century of history, setting automotive standards from the very beginning. Founded in 1916 in Munich, the company initially specialized in manufacturing aircraft engines before revolutionizing the car market with unparalleled engineering, sporty character, and elegant design. Today, BMW remains synonymous with luxury, innovation, and exceptional driving experiences, offering both classic combustion-engine models and modern electric vehicles that define the future of mobility.
